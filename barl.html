Going To The Party?

Join us at the celebration venue. Here's how to find it:

From Gorman road:

  • Turn right onto Foundry Street. You will pass an iron truss bridge on your left, followed by the historic mill.
  • Continue past the mill, through the traffic circle, then take a left, hairpin turn onto Washington Street.
  • Continue on Washington Street. It will wind left, and then left again when it dead ends to a parking lot where you can park.
  • Walk toward Savage Mill, looking for the entrance at the end of the circular driveway. Terrapin Adventures will be on your right.
  • Once in the mill, walk past the shops and follow signs for the Great Room.

Bar Mitzvah Menu
